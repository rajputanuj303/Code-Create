About the Festival

The Spring Music Festival returns for its 10th anniversary celebration. Join us for three days of incredible performances across five stages in the heart of Central Park.

This year's festival features our biggest lineup yet, with over 50 artists from around the world. From chart-topping headliners to emerging underground talent, SMF 2025 offers something for every music lover.

Beyond the music, experience art installations, gourmet food vendors, craft cocktails, and interactive activities throughout the festival grounds. Our sustainability initiatives make this our greenest event yet, with zero-waste goals and carbon-neutral operations.

Festival Highlights

  • Five unique stages with curated programming
  • Extended performance hours (12PM - 11PM daily)
  • VIP lounges with premium viewing areas
  • Family-friendly zone with activities for all ages
  • Local food vendors showcasing NYC's culinary diversity

The Spring Music Festival is committed to creating a safe, inclusive environment for all attendees. Our code of conduct ensures everyone can enjoy the festivities responsibly.
